Welding & Fabrication

Our techniques in welding and fabrication are ideally matched to most industries whether it is stainless steel welding, aluminium welding, stainless steel fabrication, aluminium fabrication, steel fabrication, non-ferrous fabrication or metal fabrication. We employ highly qualified skilled craftsmen who insist on top quality and always ensure the customer is 100% happy with the finished product.

Powder Coating

Variety of different types of Powder Coating finishes to meet the market requirements and our dynamic customers demands. We also manufacture speciality types of powder coatings as well as we offer colour matching. Our standard types of powder coatings includes: Antibacterial Powders, Architectural Powders, Epoxy Polyester Powders, Epoxy Powders, Polyester Powders, Nylon Modified Powders, Post Forming Powders.

Galvanizing

Hot dip galvanizing is a well-established and highly effective anti corrosion treatment for steel. Coatings are metallurgically bonded to the base steel, forming an impervious barrier between the substrate and the corrosive environment. Our zinc bath measures 7m x 1.3m x 2.4 mm and is capable of taking items up to three tonnes.
